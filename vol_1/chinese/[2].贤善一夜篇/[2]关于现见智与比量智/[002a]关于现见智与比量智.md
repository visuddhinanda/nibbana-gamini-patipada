## 关于现见智(paccakkha ñāṇa)与比量智(anvaya ñāṇa)

Aniccato manasikaroto adhimokkhabahulassa saddhindriyaṃ ādhipateyyaṃ hoti, bhāvanāya cattārindriyāni tadanvayā honti... (Paṭisaṃ. 245.)

**Tadanvayā hontī**ti taṃ anugāminī taṃ anuvattinī honti. (Paṭisaṃ. ṭha. 2.161.)

Paññavato bhikkhave ariyasāvakassa tadanvayā saddhā saṇṭhāti... (Saṃ. 3.195.)

**Tadanvayā**ti taṃ anugacchamānā, anuvattamānāti attho. (Saṃ. ṭha. 3.279.)

**Anvaye ñāṇa**nti cattāri saccāni paccakkhato disvā yathā idāni, evaṃ atītepi anāgatepi imeva pañcakkhandhā dukkhasaccaṃ, ayameva taṇhā samudayasaccaṃ, ayameva nirodho nirodhasaccaṃ, ayameva maggo maggasaccanti evaṃ tassa ñāṇassa anugatiyaṃ ñāṇaṃ. (Dī. ṭha. 3.202.)

**Anvaye ñāṇa**nti anugamanañāṇaṃ. **Paccakkhato disvā**ti cattāri saccāni maggañāṇena paccakkhato paṭivijjhitvā. (Dī. ṭī. 3.234.)

依照上述巴利圣典(pāli)、义注(aṭṭhakathā)、复注(ṭīkā)，比量(anvaya)有「随彼之法(tadanvaya dhamma)」与「比量智(anvaya ñāṇa)」两种。其中，**比量智**也——

- 一、紧随观智(vipassanā ñāṇa)之后而行的比量智，
- 二、紧随圣道智(ariyamagga ñāṇa)、果智(phala ñāṇa)之后而行的比量智——
