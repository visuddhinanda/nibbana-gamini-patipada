### 四十种观照角度

pañcakkhandhe aniccato, dukkhato, rogato, gaṇḍato, sallato, aghato, ābādhato, parato, palokato, ītito, upaddavato, bhayato, upasaggato, calato, pabhaṅguto, addhuvato, atāṇato, aleṇato, asaraṇato, rittato, tucchato, suññato, anattato, ādīnavato, vipariṇāmadhammato, asārakato, aghamūlato, vadhakato, vibhavato, sāsavato, saṅkhatato, mārāmisato, jātidhammato, jarādhammato, byādhidhammato, maraṇadhammato, sokadhammato, paridevadhammato, upāyāsadhammato, saṃkilesikadhammato. (Paṭisaṃ 411.) (Visuddhi 2,246.)

pañcakkhandhe aniccato passanto anulomikaṃ khantiṃ paṭilabhati. pañcannaṃ khandhānaṃ nirodho niccaṃ nibbānanti
passanto sammattaniyāmaṃ okkamati. ... (Paṭisaṃ 411.) (Visuddhi 2,246.)

在这「相之思惟智（lakkhaṇasammasanañāṇa）」部分，《清净之道》（Visuddhimagga）义注（aṭṭhakathā）引用上述《辨析道》（paṭisambhidāmagga）的那部重要巴利圣典，对四十种观照角度作了解说。
为了便于记忆这四十种观照角度，马哈维苏塔拉马大长老（Mahāvisuddhārāma Sayadaw）在其所著的**《究竟法自性辨析》（Paramatthasarūpabhedanī）**第二册第（529）页等处，以偈颂（gāthā）的形式编写串集而成。
依照那些偈颂所载，将无常相（aniccalakkhaṇa）（十）项归为一组、苦相（dukkhalakkhaṇa）（二十五）项归为一组、无我相（anattalakkhaṇa）（五）项归为一组，如此分作（三）组来书写呈现。 ---
